SDG 13: Climate Action

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Climate Targets

  • Strengthen resilience to climate-related hazards
  • Integrate climate actions into policies and planning
  • Improve awareness and education on climate issues
  • Reduce greenhouse gases with green technologies
  • Support vulnerable nations with climate financing
