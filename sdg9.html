SDG 9: Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation.

Goals & Focus

  • Develop sustainable infrastructure for economic development
  • Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization
  • Enhance scientific research and innovation in developing countries
  • Improve access to information, communications, and technology
  • Support small-scale industries with access to finance and markets
