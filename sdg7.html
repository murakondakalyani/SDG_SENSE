SDG 7: Affordable & Clean Energy

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all.

Energy Goals

  • Ensure universal access to modern energy services
  • Increase share of renewable energy in the global mix
  • Double the global rate of energy efficiency improvement
  • Enhance international cooperation for clean energy research and technology
  • Expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern energy
