SDG 12: Responsible Consumption & Production

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

Key Goals

  • Reduce food waste and overall waste generation
  • Encourage sustainable business and consumer behavior
  • Promote recycling and energy efficiency
  • Support sustainable practices across the supply chain
  • Raise awareness and educate for responsible lifestyles
