SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

Reduce inequality within and among countries.

Goals to Achieve

  • Ensure equal opportunities and reduce inequalities of outcome
  • Promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all
  • Improve regulation of financial markets and institutions
  • Ensure safe migration policies and protect migrant rights
  • Empower representation for developing countries globally
