SDG 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all.

What It Covers

  • Free primary and secondary education for all children
  • Eliminate gender disparity and ensure equal access to education
  • Promote lifelong learning and higher education scholarships
  • Improve literacy and numeracy skills in youth and adults
  • Ensure quality education facilities and trained teachers
