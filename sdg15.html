SDG 15: Life on Land

Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

Goals to Achieve

  • Protect biodiversity and natural habitats
  • Combat desertification and halt deforestation
  • Ensure the conservation of mountain ecosystems
  • Integrate ecosystem values into national planning
  • Promote fair access to land and natural resources
