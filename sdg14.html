SDG 14: Life Below Water

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources.

Marine Protection Goals

  • Prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution
  • Protect and restore coastal and marine ecosystems
  • Regulate harvesting, end overfishing and illegal fishing
  • Minimize ocean acidification and its impacts
  • Enhance marine science and ocean-related technology
